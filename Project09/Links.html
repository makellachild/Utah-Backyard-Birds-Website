Tracy Aviary

Tracy Aviary is a place where you can learn about, and interact with wild exotic and local birds!  Each day, they offer a variety of bird shows and events for all ages. Tracy Aviary's Nose to Beak Experiences allow visitors to interact with flamingos, cranes, owls, and other birds! There is absolutely nothing like it! Tracy Aviary is a great place to visit during a field trip, group tour, or even during one of their summer camps!. Tracy Aviary is located in Liberty Park, located near downtown Salt Lake City, UT.

 

Utah Birds

Utah Birds is a great place to start for new bird watchers! They have a large photo gallery and index of birds from large hawks, to small quails. Utah Birds is also a great resource for finding bird watching areas and aviary organizations across the state, and getting news about the latest bird sightings! You also have the ability to view and upload your own bird pictures in the New Bird Photos menu, and show your avaiary photography to the whole world!

Nature Tracking

Birds have footprints, too! Nature Tracking has many photos of bird tracks and wood imprints (for woodpeckers) to help bird watchers identify a certain type of bird. These photos are divided into categories based on the bird's characteristics: Classic, Game Bird, Webbed, Totipalmate, and Zygodactyl. Nature Tracking lists a brief biography, measurements, and unique facts for over 500 unique bird species!

 

Audubon

Audubon is a national society that protects birds' habitats by informing the public through news articles and photography. Their website also includes bird biographies, how-to guides for beginner bird watchers, and so much more! Audubon has several chapters and sancturaries all over the United States. Audubon has 5 chapters in Utah, two in Salt Lake City, and one in Logan, Ogden, and St. George. There is also a sancturary located in West Valley.
